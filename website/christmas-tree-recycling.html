Service Areas

Troop 202 Pickup Area (South):
  • Inside city limits of Cedar Park and Leander (South of San Gabriel River)
  • OR
  • Within 10 miles of the Cedar Park Recreation Center and no further south than FM620/SH45
  • Make checks out to "BSA Troop 202"

Check if you're within 10 miles

Troop 16/91 Pickup Area (North):
  • North of San Gabriel River
  • Liberty Hill & Georgetown
  • Make checks out to "BSA Troop 16"
Cost: $10 per tree
Cash or checks made out to "BSA Troop 202" or "BSA Troop 16" depending on your pickup area.

Important Guidelines

✅ We Can Pick Up:
  • Christmas trees stripped of all decorations
  • Trees free of tinsel
  • Trees ready by 7:30 AM
❌ We Cannot Pick Up:
  • Wreaths (due to wire frames)
  • Trees with decorations
  • Trees with tinsel
Timing Information:
  • Cedar Park addresses: Mostly morning pickups
  • Leander addresses: Mostly afternoon pickups
  • Liberty Hill: Starting in the morning
  • We cannot assign specific pickup times due to routing
Payment & Pickup:

We highly encourage leaving the tree and envelope with payment outside the night before. This helps us avoid disturbing you during pickup.
